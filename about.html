    Hi, if you don’t know already, my name is Minjoo [Min-Ju]. I'm currently based in Bay Area, but originally flew from Seoul, the capital of South Korea. In my home country, I started off as an online and offline clothing store owner. Through this experience, I learned not only how to run a business but also how to approach and communicate with customers through the brand and products. Teaching myself designs to produce all the marketing materials, I found my true passion for design and techonology.

    I believe equitable access to information and technology creates a more inclusive world, and my mission as a product designer is to acknowlege any problems that people can face while accessing those info and to produce humasnistic design solutions.

    In my ex-lives, I contributed to Onduo's design system revamp; I helped people form healthier habits and consume their daily health data through Onduo by Verily (formaly Google Life Sciences); I redesigned Project Baseline Registry;

    I love collaborating with cross functional teams, iterating through research and testing, and being involved in the growth of a product from ideation to launch.

    Let us read, and let us dance; these two amusements will never do any harm to the world.
