    Connecting the dots

    Hi, if you don’t know already, my name is Minjoo [Min-Ju]. I'm currently based in Bay Area, but originally flew from Seoul, the capital of South Korea. In my home country, I started out as an online and offline clothing store owner. Through this experience, I learned not only how to run a business but also how to approach and communicate with customers through the brand and products. Teaching myself designs to produce all the marketing materials, I learned how powerful impact design can create for business and found my true passion for design and techonology.

    My life has had constant discoveries, explorations, experiments, and enlightenments.

    I believe equitable access to information and technology creates a more inclusive world, and my mission as a product designer is to acknowledge any problems that people can face while accessing those info and to produce humanistic design solutions.

    Previously, I contributed to Onduo's design system revamp; I helped people form healthier habits and consume their health data through a mobile app Onduo; I redesigned Project Baseline Registry. Currently, I help creators protect and manage their copyrighted content, and help people share inspirations through more interactive & creative product @ Pinterst.

    When not working, you would find me hanging with my friends, travelling✈️, creating illustrations & animations✏️, playing tennis🎾, reading📚, and discovering new recipes.

    "Let us read, and let us dance; these two amusements will never do any harm to the world."
